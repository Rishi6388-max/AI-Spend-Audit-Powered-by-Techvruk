# Go-To-Market Strategy (GTM.md)

This document details the precise Go-To-Market (GTM) deployment plan designed to scale the AI Spend Audit tool from 0 to its first 100 users in 30 days with zero paid advertising budget.

---

## 1. The Exact Target User Persona

* **Job Title**: Head of Engineering / VP of Engineering or Fractional CFO / Lead Finance Partner.
* **Company Stage**: Series A to Series B startups (20 to 80 employees) who have completed initial hiring scaling and have recently set up standard expense monitoring but lack specialized DevOps or AI stack visibility.
* **The Pain Point**: They recently crossed 15+ engineers and realized their monthly bills for Claude Team, Cursor Pro, OpenAI API, and GitHub Copilot are spiking. They are under pressure from their CEO or Board to extend runway by cutting non-infrastructure software costs by 15-20% before the next fundraise.

---

## 2. Immediate Intent Identifiers (What they Google/Scroll)

Right before realizing they need this tool, our target users search or browse for:
* **Google Queries**:
  * `"Are separate Claude and ChatGPT Plus subscriptions redundant"`

  * `"Average AI developer tool budget per seat 2026"`

* **Scroll Patterns**: Clicking threads on X/Twitter discussing *"Which AI coding editor is cheapest for 30 engineers?"* or reading Y-Combinator Bookface threads asking for *"Recommended tooling limits for AI subscriptions."*

## 4. Operational Playbook: First 100 Users in 30 Days ($0 Budget)

We will bypass generic broad social posting and execute direct, hyper-targeted outreach:

1. **Days 1–10: The CFO Audit Squeeze (Target: 30 users)**:
   Scrape LinkedIn for "Fractional CFO for tech startups" in NYC/SF. Send 100 personalized direct messages offering them our clean CSV/PDF output: *"Hey [Name], your clients are likely double-paying for individual Claude Pro + ChatGPT Plus licenses. Use this free 2-minute calculator to find $12k/yr in idle software waste for your clients instantly. No email required."*
2. **Days 11–20: The "CTO Audit Tool" Community Drop (Target: 40 users)**:
   In the *Rands Leadership Slack* `#eng-ops` channel, post a high-value review: *"Hey folks—noticed our team of 25 was paying for Cursor and Copilot simultaneously, wasting $4k/yr. Built a quick open-source audit calculator to catch these seat-minimum leaks. Run your stack through it here. Zero email walls, fully open."*
3. **Days 21–30: The Product Hunt Launch (Target: 30 users)**:
   Launch on Product Hunt under a high-utility title: **"AI Spend Audit: Reclaim 30% of your AI developer stack cost in 2 minutes."** Target a Tuesday or Thursday morning slot, mobilizing our early CFO/Slack users to provide genuine review momentum.

## 5. Our Unfair Distribution Channel

**Our Unfair Channel: Partnerships with Fractional CFO Firms & Tech Venture Studio Audits.**
Instead of pitching individual startups, we will license/co-brand this tool directly to top-tier startup-focused financial firms (e.g., *Kruze Consulting*, *Pilot*, or *Bridge Bank*). We provide them with a custom white-labeled version of our tool to use as a **lead magnet** or **onboarding check** for every new Series A startup they sign. In return, they route high-volume, pre-qualified engineering stacks directly through our engine, giving us instant, high-trust distribution with zero CAC.
