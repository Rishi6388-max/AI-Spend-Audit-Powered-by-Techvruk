## 5. Frequently Asked Questions (5 Real Q&As)

### Q1: Is my company's financial data secure?
* **A**: Yes, completely. The core audit calculator runs entirely in your local browser session. We do not require or collect database credentials, financial logins, or credit card bills. If you choose to save or share your report, your identifying details (email, company name) are completely stripped from the public URL.

### Q2: Why is this audit better than asking an AI chat model?
* **A**: AI chat models frequently hallucinate pricing details or perform inaccurate calculations. Our engine uses a 100% deterministic, rules-based engine coded with official, verified pricing sheets. Our calculations are fully verifiable and finance-literate.

### Q3: What tools do you currently audit?
* **A**: We support Cursor, GitHub Copilot, Claude (Anthropic), ChatGPT (OpenAI), Windsurf, Gemini (Google), and raw OpenAI/Anthropic developer API accounts. More integrations are updated weekly.

### Q4: Do I need to create an account or sign in?
* **A**: No. We believe in providing instant value. You can run unlimited audits and view all recommendations with zero sign-ins or account gates. We only ask for an optional email if you wish to download your report or book an expert consultation.

### Q5: How does the "transactional email" delivery work?
* **A**: When you submit your email on the lead capture page, our server processes your request and automatically emails you a clean copy of your results. If your audit surfaces high savings (> $500/mo), we also flag your stack for a free, 10-minute audit review call with a Techvruk expert.
